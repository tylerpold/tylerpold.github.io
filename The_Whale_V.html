The Whale V

The Whale V is an underwater robot that I helped to construct during my senior year of highschool whilst apart of the WhaleTech Robotics team for the MATE ROV Competition. This ROV was designed to accomplish a wide range of tasks in the marine environment, specifically within the Great Lakes. To do this, the Whale V is equipped with an onboard electronics housing, a 3-Function-Manipulator, 3 High Definition (HD) deep water cameras, and 6 BlueRobotics T-200 thrusters.
